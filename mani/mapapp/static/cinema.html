Rakki Cinemas

Rakki Cinemas in Thiruvallur is a multiplex with 4 screens, 1,754 seats, 4K Dolby Atmos, and offers comfortable viewing with push-back seats and online booking. Located in Mgm Nagar, the cinema is a popular spot with high foot traffic, ample parking, and an array of amenities beyond movies like food and potentially future entertainment options.
